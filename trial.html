About Our Site

“What if we can bring our imagination into reality?” This is something the project is trying to address with the help of image style transfer and CLIP.
Image Style transfer is a technique used to take two images—a content image and a style reference image and blend them together so the output image looks like the content image, but “painted” in the style of the style reference image as shown below.


“But what if one does not have a reference image but is still interested in transferring styles by just imagining them?
To overcome the above issue, here we are using text-to-image embedding model of CLIP to perform text-driven image style transfer without the use of a style image as a reference.


Applications

“Due to the visually plausible stylised results, the research of NST has led to many successful industrial applications and begun to deliver commercial benefit.
We summarise these applications and present some potential usages.

PHOTO EDITORS

Many famous Photo Editing Apps use style transfer (but offer pre-defined or limited styling options) Eg: Prisma. Using text-style transfer we can provifde infinite styling options.

DIGITAL ART

Digital art is an artistic work that uses digital technology as part of the creative or presentation process. NST is also a form of digital art and people sell it and earn money.

HOME DECOR

Can be used by architects to produce stylized CAD drawings. Which would have been costly when created by hand. Can be used to produced stylized arts that can be displayed at homes.

ENTERTAINMENT APPLICATIONS

Can be widely used in Movies, Animations and Games. Not only can it produce visually stunning artwork, but it can also do so in a fraction of cost and time, making it highly beneficial for the entertainment industry.
